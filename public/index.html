Playlist Organizer For Spotify

A faster, more human, way of organizing which of your songs are in which of your playlists.

This application allows you to place your currently playing song into any number of your playlists, without all the menuing.

Playlist Organizer

How to:

Add a playlist to edit with below. The playlist cover art will be outlined in green if the currently playing track is in that playlist, and gray otherwise.

You can click on the album art to toggle.
